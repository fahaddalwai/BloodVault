Our Services

Safe, pure and adequate blood supply for patients in the diverse communities we serve

We cannot complete this objective without the support of the community, including support from our generous blood donors and of course, our Energetic Volunteers

What we do ?

We cut away unnecessary middlemen like blood banks and link blood donors directly with those in need, making the entire process more streamlined and effective.

Network

Savelife is one of a number of community organisations that, as part of a collaborative network, provides prompt and effective assistance in the event of an emergency.

Innovative

Savelife is one of several community organisations that, as a network, provide prompt and effective responses to unforeseen events such as natural disasters or man-made catastrophes.

What is this all about ?.

By putting patients in need of blood in direct contact with blood donors, we are able to alleviate the problem of unexpected blood shortages.

Volunteers are the real heroes

We depend on volunteers! Volunteers make up 96% of our total workforce and carry on our humanitarian work. Blood donation is healthy, our volunteers are available 24/7 to help and donate blood. Blood banks store blood bags but our volunteers are there with you in an emergency for a blood transfusion real time. Donating blood or platelets can be intimidating and even scary for many people. Time to put those hesitations and fears aside. Learn from Savelife Connect blood and platelet donors how simple and easy it is to roll up a sleeve and help save lives.

Have you given blood before?

Sometimes we prioritise appointments for regular donors. As we know their blood type, this helps us collect the right amount of each blood type to meet demand.

Your blood type

There are more appointments for donors with blood types that people often need, such as O negative. On the other hand, there are fewer appointments for blood types where demand is lower, such as AB positive.
